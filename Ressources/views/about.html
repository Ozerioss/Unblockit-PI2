About us


UnBlockIt is a student project by four engineering students from ESILV - Paris La Défense. To understand the new technology of tomorrow, we wanted to deal with the blockchain. Our curiosity pushed us to make research about it and explore it to be at the cutting edge. Most of all we want to share this knowledge with everyone and this is why Unblock It was created.
